How to play

  1. Read each challenge carefully.
  2. Discuss with your team and tap the best answer (A–D).
  3. Complete every challenge in the room — your choices are saved automatically.
  4. Open the room unlock page. If your answers are right, the room unlocks!
  5. No teacher code needed — keep going to the next room when you succeed.
  6. If unlock fails, go back, revise answers, and try again.
